CS 4400 - Phase 1
Semantic Constraints
1. Only one price may be chosen per ticket (first or second class)
2. A customer cannot view a report.
3. A customer cannot view cancelled reservations.
4. Only customer can view credit card information that he/she entered.
5. A customer may only update one ticket on a reservation at a time.
6. Refunds given to customer cannot be negative. 
7. User must enter valid train number to view reviews.
